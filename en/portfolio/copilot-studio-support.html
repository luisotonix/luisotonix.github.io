üéØ The Challenge

The client, a large organization with over 15,000 employees, faced critical challenges in internal support. The Service Desk team was overwhelmed and employees had to wait too long for simple answers.

  • High volume of repetitive tickets (password reset, system access, VPN, etc.)
  • Average response time over 4 hours for simple questions
  • Service Desk team overwhelmed with low-value tasks
  • Employees frustrated with slow IT response times
  • Technical knowledge scattered across documents, wikis and different systems
  • Difficulty scaling support without increasing headcount
  • Long-standing dream of having a "virtual assistant" for IT - previous projects failed

The differentiator: This was a project dreamed of for years by the company. Previous attempts with traditional chatbots failed due to rigidity and maintenance difficulty. Only with the evolution of Microsoft Copilot Studio was it possible to deliver a truly intelligent and scalable solution.

üí° The Solution

We implemented an intelligent virtual agent using Microsoft Copilot Studio, natively integrated with Microsoft Teams and with escalation to human agents via Dynamics 365 Customer Service.

Solution Architecture

  • Frontend: Bot published in Microsoft Teams as org-wide app
  • AI Engine: Microsoft Copilot Studio with custom topics
  • Knowledge Base: Structured topics + SharePoint integration
  • Automation: Power Automate for automated actions (e.g., password reset)
  • Escalation: Handoff to Dynamics 365 Customer Service when needed
  • Analytics: Performance dashboards and insights with Power BI

Implemented Topics

  • üîê Access: Password reset, account unlock, access requests
  • üíª Equipment: Laptop issues, printer setup, peripherals
  • üåê Connectivity: VPN, corporate Wi-Fi, remote access
  • üì¶ Software: Program installation, licenses, updates
  • üìß Email & Teams: Configuration, sync issues, permissions
  • üõ°Ô∏è Security: Phishing alerts, suspicious devices, MFA

Intelligent Service Flow

  1. Employee starts conversation with agent in Teams
  2. Copilot Studio identifies intent and searches for best response
  3. If found: responds immediately with information or executes action
  4. If not found or complex: transfers to human analyst
  5. Analyst receives full conversation context in Dynamics 365
  6. After resolution, feedback is used to train new topics

Dynamics 365 Customer Service Integration

  • Omnichannel configured to receive escalations from Copilot Studio
  • Complete conversation history transferred to analyst
  • Intelligent routing by ticket type and complexity
  • SLAs configured by priority and request type
  • Unified Knowledge Base between bot and human agents
