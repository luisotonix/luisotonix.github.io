The Challenge

The company had a legacy intranet based on SharePoint Server 2013, with an outdated interface, difficult maintenance and low employee adoption. Corporate information was scattered across multiple systems, making quick access difficult.

Store employees in particular had difficulty accessing the intranet, which was not mobile-friendly. Internal communication relied excessively on emails, resulting in inbox overload and important information being lost.

The Solution

I designed and implemented a modern intranet based on SharePoint Online, focused on user experience, mobility and integration with the Microsoft 365 ecosystem.

Phase 1: Discovery and Architecture

  • Workshops with stakeholders from all areas
  • Persona mapping and user journeys
  • Information architecture and taxonomy
  • Hub sites strategy for departments

Phase 2: Main Portal

  • Communication site as intranet home
  • Modern design with corporate branding
  • Mega menu with intuitive navigation
  • News web parts for announcements
  • Quick links to most used systems

Phase 3: Departmental Sites

  • Hub sites for HR, IT, Sales, Marketing
  • Team sites for team collaboration
  • Document libraries with metadata
  • Search configured with refiners

Phase 4: Advanced Features

  • SPFx web part for employee directory
  • Viva Connections integration
  • Power Automate for approval workflows
  • Forms for internal surveys

Phase 5: Teams Integration

  • Intranet as a tab in Teams
  • News notifications via Teams
  • Team sites connected to Teams
  • Viva Engage for communities
